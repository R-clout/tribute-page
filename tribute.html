Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu: King Of Afrofusion

The Life of Burna Boy

  • 1991 - Born in PortHaccourt, Rivers State, Nigeria.
  • 2008 to 2009 - Studied media technology at University Of Sussexx.
  • 2009 to 2010 - Studied media communications and culture at Oxford Brookes University.
  • 2013 - Burna boy released his debut studio album L.I.F.E.
  • 2014 - He spilt from Aristokrat record and founded the record label, Spaceship Entertainment.
  • 2015 - His second album, On a Spaceship, was released.
  • 2016 - His 7-track debut extended play, Redemption, was released.
  • 2018 - He released his third studio album, Outside.
  • 2019 - He won four awards at the Soundcity MVP and Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival.
  • 2019 - He also released his fourth studio album, African Giant.
  • 2020 - He released his fifth studio album, Twice as Tall.
  • 2022 - He released his sixth studio album, Love, Damini.
